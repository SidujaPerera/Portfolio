Univeristy Projects

LookOutEye

LookoutEye is an emergency app that enables users to contact the closest department in the event of an emergency. The app features three buttons that connect users with their nearest hospital, police station, or fire department. With geo-location technology, LookoutEye can easily pinpoint the user's location and connect them with the nearest emergency services. Additionally, the app includes a camera feature that enables users to communicate with a doctor at the hospital as quickly as possible. To create a seamless user experience, I used Figma to design the mobile application interfaces, and worked with a school friend to bring my vision to life. I used Android Studio to develop the code, and integrated Google Firebase for seamless data management.
